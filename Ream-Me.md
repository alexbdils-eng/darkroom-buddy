# Darkroom Buddy 🎬

Your darkroom companion app — a guided timer and reference tool for developing film and making prints.

## Features
- 🎞 **B&W Film Development** — Step-by-step timer with agitation reminders
- 🌈 **C-41 Color Processing** — Temperature-critical guided workflow  
- 🖼 **Print Making** — RC paper processing with correct Ilford times
- ⬆ **Push/Pull Processing** — Adjusted development times
- ⏱ **Free Timer** — Custom countdown for any use
- 🏆 **XP & Badges** — Gamified progression system
- 🤖 **NPC Robot Buddy** — Animated companion that follows your cursor
- 🎤 **Voice Dictation** — Hands-free note taking (Web Speech API)
- 🕶 **Meta Ray-Ban Display Ready** — 600×600 optimized

## How to Use
Visit: **https://YOUR_USERNAME.github.io/darkroom-buddy/**

Or download `index.html` and open in Chrome/Safari.

## Process References
- Film processing times based on *A Quick Guide for Developing Film* (2026)
- Print processing times based on *Making Your First Black & White Print* (Ilford/Harman)
