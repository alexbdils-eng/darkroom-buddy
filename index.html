CHEMISTRY GUIDE 📖

DEVELOPER 🧪

Converts exposed silver halide crystals into metallic silver. Time and temperature are critical — overdevelopment increases contrast and grain.

STOP BATH 🛑

Halts development by neutralizing the developer. 30 seconds with gentle agitation is enough.

FIXER 🔧

Removes unexposed silver halide, making the image permanent. Under-fixing leaves a milky/purple cast. Film needs ~5 min.

HYPO CLEAR 💧

Shortens washing time by aiding removal of fixer. Cuts final wash from 30 min to 5-10 min.

PHOTO-FLO 🫧

Minimizes water spots and streaks during drying. One drop per tank. Do NOT agitate — let it sit perfectly still.

TEMPERATURE & TIME 🌡

Standard: 68°F / 20°C. Each 1°F above reduces time ~10%. Below 65°F chemistry becomes sluggish.

AGITATION 🔄

Inversion: Flip 4x in 5s, every 30-60s. Rotary: Continuous in Jobo, reduce times ~15%. Tray: Rock corner-to-corner for prints.

PUSH / PULL ⬆

Push (+1 stop) ≈ +30% time, more grain & contrast. Pull (−1 stop) ≈ −20% time, less contrast. Best within ±2 stops.
